# How to Reset Your Password

## Overview

This article explains how to reset your TaskFlow account password if you cannot log in.

## Problem

You are unable to log in because you forgot your password or your password is not working.

## Solution

To reset your password:

1. Open the TaskFlow login page.
2. Click Forgot Password.
3. Enter your registered email address.
4. Click Submit.
5. Check your email for the password reset link.
6. Click the link and enter a new password.
7. Log in using your new password.

## Additional Notes

- The reset link is valid for a limited time.
- If you do not receive the email, check your spam folder.
