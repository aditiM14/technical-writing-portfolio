# How to Verify Your Email

## Overview

This article explains how to verify your email after you create a TaskFlow account.

## Problem

You need to verify your email to complete account creation.

## Solution

To verify your email:

1. Open the verification email sent to your inbox.
2. Click the verification link.
3. Wait for the authentication process to complete.
4. Try logging in to confirm your account is activated.

## Additional Notes

- Ensure you entered the correct email address.
- If the email is not received, check your spam or junk folder.
