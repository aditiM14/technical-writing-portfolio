# How to Invite Team Members

## Overview

This article explains how to invite team members to collaborate on a project in TaskFlow.

## Problem

You want to add other users to your project so they can view or manage tasks.

## Solution

To invite a team member:

1. Open the project you want to share.
2. Click Invite.
3. Enter the email address of the user.
4. Click Send Invitation.

The invited user will receive an email with instructions to join the project.

## Additional Notes

- Make sure the email address is correct.
- Invitations may expire if not accepted within a certain time.
