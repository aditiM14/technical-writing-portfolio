# After Version - TaskFlow Installation Guide

## 1. Introduction

TaskFlow is a web-based task management application that helps you organize and track tasks and projects.

This guide explains how to set up your TaskFlow account and start using the application.

## 2. System Requirements

To use TaskFlow, ensure you have:

- A modern web browser (Google Chrome, Firefox, or Microsoft Edge)
- An active internet connection

No installation is required because TaskFlow runs in your browser.

## 3. Set Up Your Account

### 3.1 Create an Account

To create a TaskFlow account:

1. Open your web browser and go to the TaskFlow website.
2. Click **Sign Up**.
3. Enter your full name.
4. Enter your email address.
5. Create a password (minimum 8 characters).
6. Click **Create Account**.

You will receive a verification email after signing up.

### 3.2 Verify Your Email

To verify your account:

1. Open the verification email sent to your inbox.
2. Click the verification link.

Your account will be activated after verification.

### 3.3 Log In

To log in to TaskFlow:

1. Open the TaskFlow website.
2. Click **Log In**.
3. Enter your registered email address.
4. Enter your password.
5. Click **Log In**.

After logging in, you will be redirected to the dashboard.

## 4. Troubleshooting Setup Issues

### Issue: Verification email not received

Try the following:

- Check your spam or junk folder.
- Ensure you entered the correct email address.
- Wait a few minutes and refresh your inbox.

### Issue: Unable to log in

Try the following:

- Check that your email and password are correct.
- Use **Forgot Password** to reset your password.

### Issue: Website not loading

Try the following:

- Check your internet connection.
- Refresh the page.
- Try a different browser.

## 5. FAQ

### What is TaskFlow?

TaskFlow is a web-based application that helps you manage tasks and projects.

### Do I need to install TaskFlow?

No. TaskFlow runs in your browser and does not require installation.

### Why am I not receiving the verification email?

Check your spam folder and confirm that you entered the correct email address. If the issue continues, try signing up again.

### What should I do if I cannot log in?

Make sure your email and password are correct. If needed, click **Forgot Password** to reset your password.
